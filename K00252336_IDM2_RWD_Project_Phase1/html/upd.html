Every year around this time, Valve comes out with a complete overhaul, disrupting the existing meta. This year, players will have to wait around just a little longer for these changes to come into the system.

However, earlier today, Valve put out a statement which said that the new patch will arrive along with a brand-new female hero sometime in mid-December.

The key aspect of this update is that it will improve on the existing cheater identification system. With these changes, players will now be able to review matches in which they feel smurfing was existing. This will also help eliminate toxicity in-game, especially in lower brackets.
